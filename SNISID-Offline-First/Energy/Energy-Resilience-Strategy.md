# National Energy Resilience Strategy

## Layered Approach
- **National Core Datacenters**: UPS + diesel generators (72h minimum)
- **Regional Edge Nodes**: Solar + lithium batteries (7+ days autonomy)
- **Mobile Units**: Portable solar + battery banks
- **Communications**: Satellite terminals with independent power

## National Blackout Survival
- All critical nodes maintain minimum 72-hour operation
- Regional nodes target 14-day autonomy
- Prioritized power allocation to identity services

## Monitoring
- Battery health telemetry (when connected)
- Solar generation logs
- Automatic low-power mode activation
