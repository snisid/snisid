# National Crisis Mode Operations

## Activated During
- Major natural disasters
- Prolonged internet blackout
- Political instability affecting infrastructure

## Enhanced Capabilities
- Emergency identity issuance (temporary documents)
- Prioritized workflows for humanitarian aid
- Reinforced regional autonomy (no sync required)
- Degraded sync mode (only critical data)
- Offline judicial operations (local decisions logged)

## Governance
- Crisis mode activated by designated national authority
- Automatic fallback after 48h of core unavailability
- Full audit of all crisis-mode actions
