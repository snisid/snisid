# SNISID: Strategic Geopolitical Simulation Engine

The Strategic Geopolitical Simulation Engine provides the "Sovereign War Room" for modeling large-scale structural threats and geopolitical crises that could impact the national identity infrastructure.

---

## 1. War Gaming & Structural Threat Modeling

The engine simulates "Black Swan" events that transcend simple technical failures.

- **Mass Document Forgery Simulation**: Modeling the impact of a coordinated campaign to inject millions of high-quality forged documents into the national registry.
- **Regional Isolation Scenarios**: Modeling the operational impact of a total loss of connectivity to a specific regional spoke or a neighboring country's data exchange.
- **Biometric Database Poisoning**: Simulating a scenario where an adversary successfully alters facial recognition templates for a subset of the population.

---

## 2. Geopolitical Risk Integration

The engine integrates real-time geopolitical telemetry to adjust its simulation parameters.

- **International Sanctions Modeling**: Modeling the impact of technical sanctions on infrastructure components (e.g., loss of support for specific software/hardware).
- **Border Tension Dynamics**: Automatically increasing the difficulty of "Identity Verification" in simulations involving border-crossing events during periods of high geopolitical friction.
- **Global Identity Standards Shift**: Modeling the technical effort required to transition the national infrastructure to a new international identity standard.

---

## 3. Simulation Architecture

- **The Sovereign Sandbox**: A high-performance compute cluster (GPU/CPU) isolated from the production mesh.
- **Monte Carlo Resilience Engine**: Runs millions of probabilistic scenarios to identify the "Fracture Points" in national security policies.
- **AI-Driven Adversaries**: Specialized AI agents that mimic the TTPs of state-sponsored threat actors in a simulated environment.

---

## 4. Strategic Planning & Policy Testing

- **Policy Stress Testing**: Allowing policy-makers to "Play Out" the consequences of a new law or technical regulation (e.g., *"What happens if we mandate 3D facial scanning for all citizens in 6 months?"*).
- **Crisis Resource Planning**: Identifying the optimal distribution of physical and digital security assets during a simulated national emergency.
- **Resilience Certification**: Providing a "Sovereign Resilience Score" for every major architectural change before it is deployed to production.

---

## 5. Security & Governance

- **Air-Gapped Execution**: Simulations involving sensitive national security parameters are run on physically air-gapped infrastructure.
- **Redaction of Real Data**: All simulations use **Synthetically Generated Citizens** and infrastructure models, ensuring that zero real citizen PII is used in the War Room.
- **Oversight Audit**: Every simulation is logged, including the parameters used, the scenarios played out, and the conclusions reached, for review by the National Security Council.
