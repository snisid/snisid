🧾 TITLE

SNISID – National Digital Identity and Security Infrastructure Program

🌍 1. CONTEXT

Haïti faces structural constraints in:

fragmented civil registry systems
limited interoperability across public institutions
high levels of identity fraud
inefficient fiscal and administrative systems

This results in reduced trust in public institutions and weakened governance capacity.

🎯 2. OBJECTIVE

The SNISID program aims to establish a national Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI) consisting of:

a unified digital identity system
a secure inter-agency data exchange layer
a national cybersecurity SOC
AI-assisted fraud detection systems
🏗️ 3. PROPOSED SOLUTION
Core Components:
National Digital Identity Registry
Secure API Gateway for ANH / DGI / DCPJ
SOC (Security Operations Center)
Real-time data streaming backbone (Kafka)
Graph-based identity system (Neo4j)
AI fraud detection (biometrics + anomaly detection)

📄 2) EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (1 PAGE — PRESIDENT / MINISTER)
🧾 SNISID – NATIONAL DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION PROGRAM

The SNISID program establishes a sovereign digital infrastructure designed to modernize Haiti’s identity management, strengthen institutional coordination, and enhance national security through secure data systems and artificial intelligence.

This initiative introduces a unified national identity system, enabling secure verification across public institutions, including civil registry, taxation, and judicial services.

SNISID integrates a national cybersecurity operations center and advanced fraud detection capabilities to reduce administrative inefficiencies and improve trust in public services.

The program is structured in phased deployment stages, beginning with a controlled pilot and scaling to full national implementation.

Key benefits include:

improved fiscal efficiency
reduction in identity fraud
enhanced inter-agency coordination
strengthened digital governance

SNISID aligns with international standards promoted by the World Bank ID4D initiative and the United Nations digital governance frameworks.

This program represents a foundational step toward a modern, secure, and interoperable digital state infrastructure.
