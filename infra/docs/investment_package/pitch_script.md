# SNISID: Investment Pitch Script
## Oral Presentation for WB / IMF Investment Committee

---

### 1. The Hook (0:00 - 1:00)
"Honorable members of the Committee, thank you for your time.
We are not here to propose a software application. We are here to propose the **digital foundation of a nation**.
In our current landscape, fragmented identity systems are the single greatest barrier to economic growth and institutional trust. SNISID is the bridge that solves this."

### 2. The Problem (1:00 - 2:30)
"Today, our governance is inefficient not because of a lack of will, but because of a lack of data integrity. When 60% of your population is digitally invisible, you cannot deliver healthcare, you cannot collect taxes fairly, and you cannot stop systemic fraud. In Haiti, we estimate that identity fraud costs our treasury hundreds of millions of dollars every year—funds that should be building schools and hospitals."

### 3. The Solution (2:30 - 4:00)
"SNISID—the National Intelligent System for Identity & Document Security—is a sovereign digital public infrastructure.
It provides a single, biometric-backed truth for every citizen. It uses artificial intelligence to detect fraud before it happens, and it uses a sovereign cyber-defense layer to ensure that this data remains the property of the state and its people. This is built on a Zero-Trust architecture, meaning security is baked into every line of code."

### 4. Governance & Trust (4:00 - 5:30)
"We understand the sensitivity of national data. This is why SNISID is built on international standards. We are fully aligned with the World Bank’s ID4D principles and the GDPR. Our governance model includes an independent authority with international oversight. We have implemented immutable audit logs—think of it as a black box for government operations—where every data access is recorded and cannot be altered."

### 5. The Ask & ROI (5:30 - 7:00)
"We are seeking a strategic investment of $45 million for a 3-year phased national rollout.
The ROI is not just social—it is financial. By reducing identity fraud by even 20%, the system pays for itself within the first 18 months.
But more importantly, SNISID provides the rails upon which the entire digital economy will run—from fintech to e-government."

### 6. Closing (7:00 - 7:30)
"SNISID is about more than technology; it is about **trust**. It is about giving every citizen a voice and every institution the tools to serve them.
We invite you to partner with us in building the digital state of the future.
Thank you."
