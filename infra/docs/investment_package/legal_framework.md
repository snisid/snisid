# SNISID: Legal & Compliance Framework
## Regulatory Annexes for International Investment (WB/IMF/UNDP)

---

### Annex A: Data Protection & Privacy (GDPR/ID4D Alignment)
SNISID is built on the "Privacy by Design" principle, ensuring compliance with global standards.
- **Data Minimization:** Only essential biometric and demographic tokens are stored.
- **Crypto-Shredding:** PII is encrypted with keys stored in HashiCorp Vault; deletion of keys ensures immutable right-to-be-forgotten.
- **Consent Management:** Citizen-led authorization for inter-agency data sharing.

### Annex B: The SNISID Authority Act
Proposed legislation for an independent governing body.
- **Mandate:** Oversight of national identity standards and cyber-defense.
- **Structure:** Board composition including government, private sector, and international observers (WB/UN).
- **Auditability:** Mandatory annual external technical and financial audits.

### Annex C: Critical Infrastructure Protection Law
Establishing SNISID as a "Tier-1 National Asset."
- **Cybersecurity Standards:** Compulsory adherence to NIST/ISO 27001.
- **Incident Reporting:** Legal requirement for immediate disclosure of breaches to the national SOC.
- **Sovereignty Clause:** Data residency restricted to sovereign soil or hardware-attested diplomatic clouds.

### Annex D: AI Governance & Ethics
Framework for automated decision-making.
- **Explainability (XAI):** Requirement for human-readable audit trails for all AI-driven fraud flags.
- **Human-in-the-Loop:** High-risk decisions (e.g., identity revocation) require manual review by a certified officer.
- **Bias Monitoring:** Continuous auditing of algorithms for demographic neutrality.
