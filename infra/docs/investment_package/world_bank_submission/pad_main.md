# Project Appraisal Document (PAD): SNISID National Program
## Sovereign National Identity & Security Infrastructure Development

---

### 1. PROJECT INFORMATION
- **Project Name:** SNISID — National Digital Identity and Security Infrastructure Program
- **Country:** Haiti
- **Sector:** Digital Development / Governance / Public Administration Reform
- **Instrument:** Investment Project Financing (IPF)
- **Total Financing:** $65,000,000 (Phased)
- **Implementing Agency:** National SNISID Authority (NAS)

### 2. PROJECT DEVELOPMENT OBJECTIVE (PDO)
To establish a high-assurance, secure, and interoperable national digital identity and data governance infrastructure to enhance public service delivery efficiency, reduce identity-based fiscal fraud, and strengthen national cybersecurity resilience.

### 3. PROJECT COMPONENTS

#### Component 1: Sovereign Identity Infrastructure ($25M)
- Implementation of a biometric-backed national identity registry.
- Deployment of mobile-first enrollment kits and remote verification APIs.
- Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI) compliant identity lifecycle management.

#### Component 2: Inter-Agency Data Exchange Layer ($15M)
- Creation of a secure, mTLS-encrypted national API gateway.
- Real-time event backbone (Kafka/Flink) for cross-institution synchronization.
- Integration with Tax (DGI), Police (DCPJ), and Vital Records (ONI).

#### Component 3: Autonomous SOC & Cyber Command ($15M)
- 24/7 Security Operations Center (SOC) with AI-driven threat hunting.
- Deployment of an autonomous SOAR (Security Orchestration, Automation, and Response) layer.
- Hardened sovereign cloud infrastructure on Kubernetes.

#### Component 4: Institutional Capacity & Governance ($10M)
- Establishing the National SNISID Authority and judicial oversight board.
- Comprehensive technical training for local government staff.
- Legal framework implementation (GDPR/ID4D alignment).

### 4. IMPLEMENTATION ARRANGEMENTS
- **Steering Committee:** Chaired by the Prime Minister's Office, including Ministers of Finance, Justice, and Interior.
- **Project Management Unit (PMU):** Responsible for daily operations, financial management, and WB procurement compliance.
- **External Audit:** Semi-annual independent audits of both financial records and cybersecurity posture.

### 5. MONITORING & EVALUATION (M&E)
- **Outcome 1:** 90% adult population enrollment within 36 months.
- **Outcome 2:** 30% reduction in public sector payroll/pension fraud.
- **Outcome 3:** Sub-200ms identity verification response time across all agency endpoints.
