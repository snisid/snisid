# SNISID: Implementation Supervision Framework
## Governance, Monitoring, and Institutional Oversight

---

### 1. Supervision Hierarchy
- **Level 1: The Board (World Bank / IMF):** Strategic oversight, tranche approvals, and global policy alignment.
- **Level 2: Steering Committee (Haiti):** Chaired by PM, ensures inter-ministerial cooperation.
- **Level 3: SNISID Authority (NAS):** The executing agency managing technical and operational deliverables.

### 2. Monitoring Mechanisms
- **Quarterly Supervision Missions:** In-person and remote technical audits by World Bank specialists.
- **Real-Time KPI Dashboard:** A dedicated portal providing the World Bank with live metrics on:
    - Enrollment rates vs. targets.
    - System uptime and latency.
    - Security incident volume and response time.
    - Financial disbursement vs. milestone completion.

### 3. Reporting Requirements
- **Monthly Progress Reports:** Technical and financial status submitted by the PMU.
- **Semi-Annual Audit Reports:** Produced by an independent international audit firm.
- **Mid-Term Review:** Comprehensive project assessment at the 18-month mark.

### 4. Quality Assurance (QA) Framework
- **Code Audit:** Regular review of the Go backend and Kubernetes manifests for security vulnerabilities.
- **Penetration Testing:** Annual "Red Team" exercises simulating national-scale attacks.
- **Ethics Review:** Annual audit of AI models for bias and compliance with sovereign governance rules.
