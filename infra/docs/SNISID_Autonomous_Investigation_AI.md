# SNISID: Autonomous Investigation AI

The Autonomous Investigation AI layer provides "Deep Intelligence" capabilities, allowing the system to autonomously reconstruct complex, multi-vector security events for national inquiry and forensic preservation.

---

## 1. Advanced Investigation Agents

These agents are the "Elite Detectives" of the **Sovereign SOC Swarm**, specializing in multi-domain forensics.

### 1.1. Agent Specializations
- **The Graph Pathologist**: Specializes in reconstructing complex relationship chains in Neo4j to identify the "Root Cause" of a community-wide compromise.
- **The Binary Forensic Analyst**: Performs automated reverse engineering and behavior analysis of malicious code samples captured during an incident.
- **The Identity Auditor**: Reconstructs the entire lifecycle of a suspicious identity, from initial issuance to the point of compromise, looking for technical or administrative failures.

---

## 2. Event Reconstruction & Timeline Mapping

The AI agents build a **Consolidated Incident Timeline** by fusing data from all national topics.

- **Chronological Synthesis**: Linking a low-level syscall alert (HIDS) with a high-level API request and a corresponding change in the identity graph.
- **Evidence Weighting**: Assigning a "Certainty Score" to each link in the investigation chain.
- **Visual Reconstruction**: Generating interactive 4D maps (Time + Space + Relationship) of an incident for SOC analysts and judicial investigators.

---

## 3. Sovereign Forensic Workbench

- **Isolated Evidence Vault**: All investigation artifacts (memory dumps, logs, graph slices) are stored in an encrypted, air-gapped forensic vault.
- **Reproducible Analysis**: The system can "Re-Run" the investigation on the same data to verify the AI's conclusions, ensuring judicial-grade non-repudiation.
- **Collaboration API**: Allows human investigators to "Chat" with the AI agents to request specific analyses or deeper dives into evidence subsets.

---

## 4. Operational Workflows

- **Auto-Investigation Trigger**: Any "Critical" (SSS > 800) alert automatically launches an Investigation Swarm.
- **Hypothesis Generation**: The AI generates multiple "Attack Scenarios" and attempts to prove or disprove them based on the available telemetry.
- **Final Report Generation**: Automatically produces a comprehensive **Sovereign Investigation Report (SIR)**, including the attack vector, blast radius, and remediation recommendations.

---

## 5. Security & Judicial Compliance

- **Chain of Custody**: Every piece of digital evidence is cryptographically hashed and logged to the **Sovereign Audit Ledger** at the moment of collection.
- **Privacy Enforcement**: The investigation AI is strictly prohibited from accessing certain "Protected Fields" (e.g., medical records) without an explicit digital warrant from the Ministry of Justice.
- **Non-Repudiation**: All AI-generated conclusions are digitally signed by the **Sovereign Intelligence Root Key**.
