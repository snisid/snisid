# SNISID Deployment Roadmap: Republic of Haiti (2026-2029)

## Phase 1: Foundation (0-6 Months)
**Objective**: Establish core sovereign identity backbone.
- **Milestones**: 
  - Provisioning of the Port-au-Prince Primary Data Center.
  - Migration of the ANH civil registry to SNISID Postgres/Neo4j.
  - Deployment of the Identity Mesh (v1).
- **Budget**: $2.5M - $5.0M USD.

## Phase 2: Inter-Agency Federation (6-18 Months)
**Objective**: Connect DGI and DCPJ into the unified intelligence mesh.
- **Milestones**: 
  - Bi-directional sync with Tax (DGI) and Criminal (DCPJ) records.
  - Activation of the Global SOC Command Center.
  - Implementation of the multi-agency fraud scoring engine.
- **Budget**: $5.0M - $12.0M USD.

## Phase 3: National Scaling & Resilience (18-36 Months)
**Objective**: Achieve total national coverage and disaster recovery.
- **Milestones**: 
  - Secondary Data Center (Cap-Haïtien) for high-availability.
  - Full roll-out of biometric verification kits to national border points.
  - Implementation of predictive threat forecasting.
- **Budget**: $10.0M - $25.0M USD.

## Phase 4: AI Autonomy & Optimization (36+ Months)
**Objective**: Self-healing governance and advanced AI intelligence.
- **Milestones**: 
  - Deployment of the Cyber Singularity Brain for global optimization.
  - Federated learning across regional security clusters.
  - Autonomous SOC self-healing via GOS.
- **Budget**: $5.0M - $15.0M USD.
